What should I do if I lose an item?

If you lose an item on campus, first **report it** through our "Submit Item" page. Provide as much detail as possible, including where and when you last saw it, and a clear description. Then, regularly check the "Found Items" list on our portal.

How do I report an item I've found?

Thank you for helping! Go to the "Submit Item" page, select "Found" as the item type, and fill in the details about the item. You can either hold onto the item or drop it off at the main campus security office, as specified in your post.

How can I claim my lost item?

If you see your item listed in the "Found" section, click on it and follow the instructions to contact the finder or the admin. You will need to provide specific details about the item to prove ownership before arranging to collect it.
